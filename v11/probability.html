Basic Probability

Probability Axioms

0 ≤ P(A) ≤ 1, P(S) = 1 for sample space S, and P(A∪B) = P(A) + P(B) for mutually exclusive events.

Independent vs Dependent Events

Independent: P(A∩B) = P(A) × P(B). Dependent: P(A|B) ≠ P(A).

Addition and Multiplication Rules

Addition: P(A∪B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A∩B). Multiplication: P(A∩B) = P(A|B) × P(B).

Bayes' Theorem

Updating Beliefs

P(H|E) = P(E|H) × P(H) / P(E) - posterior = likelihood × prior / evidence
