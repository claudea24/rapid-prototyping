What are Case Studies?

Case studies are like real-life mysteries where you use data to solve business problems. It's detective work with numbers!

Common Frameworks

  • Product Metrics: Track how users interact with products
  • A/B Testing: Compare different versions
  • Cohort Analysis: Study groups over time
  • Funnel Analysis: See where users drop off

Key Steps in Case Studies

  1. Understand the Problem: What exactly needs to be solved?
  2. Gather Data: Find relevant information
  3. Analyze: Look for patterns and insights
  4. Recommend: Suggest solutions with evidence
  5. Present: Explain your findings clearly
