What is Probability?

Probability is about how likely something is to happen. It's like guessing the weather or picking lottery numbers, but with math!

Basic Concepts

  • Probability: A number between 0 and 1 (0% to 100%)
  • Events: Things that can happen
  • Independent: One event doesn't affect another
  • Dependent: Events affect each other

Key Concepts

  • Expected Value: What you expect to happen on average
  • Bayes' Theorem: Updating beliefs with new information
  • Distributions: How probabilities are spread out
  • Conditional Probability: Probability given some condition
